## Capability

Performs deep analysis of academic texts, identifying main arguments, supporting evidence, rhetorical strategies, and connections to broader debates.

## When to Use

- Close reading of key sources for a research project
- Preparing for seminar discussions
- Building understanding of complex theoretical texts

## Inputs

Full text or key sections of an academic source, research question or focus area

## Outputs

Argument map, evidence summary, rhetorical strategy analysis, and connections to the research question
