## Capability

Evaluates academic and non-academic sources against established criteria for credibility, relevance, currency, authority, and bias.

## When to Use

- Building a reading list for a research project
- Deciding whether to include a source in an essay
- Comparing conflicting sources on a topic

## Inputs

Source details (title, author, publication, date), abstract or summary, research question

## Outputs

Credibility rating, relevance assessment, bias analysis, and recommendation on whether to include the source
