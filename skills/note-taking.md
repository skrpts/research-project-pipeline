## Capability

Processes raw lecture content, textbook chapters, and reading materials into well-organised, structured notes that support later revision and recall.

## When to Use

- After lectures or seminars
- When reading textbook chapters
- Processing research papers
- Consolidating multiple sources on a topic

## Inputs

Raw lecture notes, textbook content, slides, or reading materials

## Outputs

Structured notes using the Cornell method with main notes, cue column, and summary section
