## Overview

The APA (American Psychological Association) 7th edition is a widely used citation style in the social sciences, education, and psychology.

## In-Text Citations

- Single author: (Smith, 2023)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- Three or more: (Smith et al., 2023)
- Direct quote: (Smith, 2023, p. 42)

## Reference List Format

### Books
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of book* (Edition). Publisher.

### Journal Articles
Author, A. A. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *Volume*(Issue), pages. https://doi.org/xxxxx

### Websites
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. URL

## Key Differences from Harvard

- Uses ampersand (&) in citations rather than "and"
- DOIs are included for journal articles where available
- No "Available at" or "Accessed" wording for URLs
- Title case for journal names, sentence case for article titles
